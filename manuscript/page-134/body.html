"Hast thou heard what Garselit sung, The Irishman whom it is safe to follow? Sin is bad, if long pursued."

"Hast thou heard what Avaon sung, The son of Taliesin, of the recording verse? The cheek will not conceal the anguish of the heart."

"Didst thou hear what Llywarch sung, The intrepid and brave old man? Greet kindly, though there be no acquaintance."
